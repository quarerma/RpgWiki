**Elminster** is one of the most powerful wizards in all of Faerûn, known for his wisdom, wit, and signature pointed hat.

## History

Elminster was born in the Dalelands and has lived for centuries, witnessing the rise and fall of empires. He serves as a Chosen of Mystra, the goddess of magic.

## Relationships

Elminster is closely tied to [[Shadowdale]], where he has long been a protector of the realm. He has crossed paths with many adventurers, including the famous drow ranger [[Drizzt Do'Urden]].

## Abilities

Elminster is a master of _arcane magic_ and has access to spells far beyond the reach of ordinary mages. His knowledge of the Weave is nearly unparalleled.
