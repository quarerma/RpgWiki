**Shadowdale** is a small but storied community in the Dalelands of central Faerûn.

## Overview

Shadowdale is known for its pastoral beauty and its long association with powerful wizards. The town has weathered many threats over the centuries thanks to its protectors.

## Notable residents

The legendary wizard [[Elminster]] has deep ties to Shadowdale and is often associated with the region.

## Geography

Shadowdale sits among rolling hills and farmland, connected to the wider Dalelands by well-traveled roads. Adventurers passing through often hear tales of [[Drizzt Do'Urden]] and other heroes who have visited the area.
