**Drizzt Do'Urden** is a drow ranger who rejected the evil ways of his people and chose to live on the surface world.

## Background

Born in the underground city of Menzoberranzan, Drizzt fled his homeland after refusing to participate in the cruel traditions of drow society.

## Companions

Drizzt is known for his loyal companions, including his panther Guenhwyvar. He has traveled extensively across the *Sword Coast* and the North.

## Notable encounters

Though Drizzt operates independently, his path has occasionally intersected with legendary figures such as [[Elminster]].
